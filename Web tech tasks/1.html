MOHAN BABU UNIVERSITY

University in Andhra Pradesh, India

Mohan Babu University (MBU) is a private university located in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh.[3] Established in 1992, formerly known as Sree Vidyanikethan Educational Institutions[4] and currently as Mohan Babu University since January 2022,[5] it consists of nine schools, offering academic programs at Undergraduate (UG), Postgraduate (PG), and Ph.D courses in engineering, liberal arts and basic sciences, agricultural, paramedical, commerce, media and film-academy based focused courses.[6]
